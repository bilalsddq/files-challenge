Elena Gilbert
Elena Gilbert Elena Gilbert is a star student, popular, sporty, smart, compassionate, empathetic, caring and friendly. She can be vulnerable and a damsel in distress. She is a normal human, until she finds out that she is a Petrova doppelganger, but later turns into a vampire, and then back into a human later on.

Elena appears in both the CW show and book called The Vampire Diaries
